Atif Aslam

Atif Aslam is one of the biggest and most popular playback singer in the Bollywood Entertainment Industry. He has recorded numerous chart-topping songs and is known for his vocal belting technique. He predominantly sings in Urdu, Hindi and Punjabi, but has also sung in Bengali.
